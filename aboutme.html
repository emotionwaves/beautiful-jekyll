I am Roza Kamiloglu, a PhD candidate in psychology at University of Amsterdam where I developed my fascination for emotional vocalizations.

A puzzle has occupied me since my undergraduate studies - the human ability to communicate emotions via sensory systems in social context. In my PhD project, under the supervision of Disa Sauter and Agneta Fischer, I am working on nonverbal vocal expressions associated with specific positive emotions across cultures and the lifespan. We will share related research output in this website.
