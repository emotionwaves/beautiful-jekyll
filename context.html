Distribution of different behavioral contexts in acoustic space of chimpanzee vocalizations.
c1 = discovering something scary, c2 = copulating (having sex), c3 = being tickled, c4 = discovering a large food source, c5 = eating high value food, c6 = eating low value food, c7 = being refused to food access, c8 = being attacked by another chimp, c9 = threatening an aggressive chimp, c10 = being separated from mother.
